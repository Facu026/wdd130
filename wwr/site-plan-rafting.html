Overview

Purpose

In Good Vibes our main purpose is to prepare the customers for all our amazing trips to white water rafting. You will have the chance to select a variety of activities and things to do here and don't worry if it is your first time doing water rafting. We are offering you a very rich description of everything and the chance to make a purchase online for your tickets here. And at the same time, we are offering you the best tips about what to wear, what to eat, and much more. Remember we are here to help you so don't let your questions without an answer! We have our customer support ready to answer your questions in less than 24hs! Why you should choose us? Easy, we know that you probably are struggling with college or with work stuff, so Good Vibes Whitewater Rafting is a family business that is focused on helping everyone especially students to get an amazing adventure during their holidays and weekends because we are working these days to you. Our little new business has a lot of reviews from previous customers that enjoyed to spent their time with us, feel free to look at their positive comments. Another reason to choose us is that we really take care of the environment by cleaning the place every day and recycling all the trash, and maintaining everything as natural as possible to make you feel a real adventure on the hikes, whitewater rafting, and much more.

Audience

Our target customers are young people between 18 to 35 years old. They are studying at university, most of them have a part-time jobs and they want to enjoy weekends with their friends and try new things. They usually think that white water rafting is too expensive to try, so my website will help them to know more about it. They will usually access my site on their mobile devices.
